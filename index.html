Facts

The Internet is a system of computers connected through a Transmission Control Protocol. This allows different devices to communicate with each other with speed and efficiency.
It was started in 1983 by American computer scientists Bob Kahn and Vint Cerf.
The internet is still widely used today!

The World Wide Web (commonly known as www) is an easy-access information system on the internet.
The world wide web was considered more user-friendly to those who were not computer experts. It can be credited with bringing the internet into the lives of everyday citizens around the globe.
